---
title: "Garam masala"
description: "A recipe for garam masala."
created: 2020-01-01
---

- 2 tbsp coriander seed
- 1 tsp cumin seed
- 0.5 tsp whole cloves
- 0.5 tsp cardamom seeds
- 2 bay leaves
- 3 dried red chilis
- 2 inches cinnamon

Grind all ingredients to a coarse powder (easiest with a coffee grinder or large
mortar and pestle).
